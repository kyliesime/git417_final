About Me

Hi, I’m Shayla!

I am the owner and Head Coach of Shayla Rae Coaching, a coaching program designed to help women become the best versions of themselves. Growing up, I never felt as though I was good enough and struggled with my confidence to a high degree. The negative image I had of myself caused me to believe that I wasn’t capable of achieving great things, and I fell into a self deprecating headspace throughout my high school years. I discovered weight lifting when I was about 15 years old, but didn’t take it seriously for a couple years due to a lack of knowledge. I was intrigued by the idea of it, so I got a job at a gym and slowly learned about lifting and nutrition between the ages of 16-18. I had no idea how truly life changing this lifestyle would become for me, but once I learned and grew as an athlete, I discovered that I had a sense of confidence in myself that I had never known before.

Through speaking with so many women, I realized that I had not been the only one who was massively lacking confidence and self worth and I knew that this lifestyle could help other women change their lives in the same way that it had for me. Soon after, I became a CPT and was on a mission to help inspire women to take the time to work on their physical health, focus on personal growth, and help them realize the potential that had been inside them all along. My biggest goal through my coaching program is to help women see that they are capable of achieving so much more than they ever thought was possible through strength training and nutrition coaching.
